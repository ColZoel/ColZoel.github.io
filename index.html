Collin Zoeller

Computer Vision Developer, Economics Resesarcher, and Data Scientist

Well howdy there!

I have my B.S. in economics and am currently a pre-doctoral research associate at the Tepper School of Business at Carnegie Mellon University, where I work on a variety of projects related to financial economics, machine learning, and data science.

I am interested in the applications of ML in economics, and am formally the lead computer vision developer at the Record Linking Lab at Brigham Young University, building CV tools for genealogy. I am also interested in the economics of black markets, immigration, crime, and labor.

My technical experience is primarily self-taught. I have most experience in Python and Stata, and am familiar with R and SQL with some HTML and CSS (as you can see from this website).

I am always excited to take on new projects, so feel free to reach out if you are interested in working together!
